## Design Context

**Users**
Language learners using flashcards to study and review decks. They need rapid resume-to-learning flows and focused study sessions with zero friction.

**Brand Personality**
Apple-inspired, calm, premium, and restrained. The feeling is of a high-quality editorial learning product rather than a generic utility or noisy AI-SaaS application.

**Aesthetic Direction**
- Layouts must prioritize clarity, restraint, and exceptionally strong readability.
- Depth is built through generous spacing, subtle surface contrast, and established structural hierarchy rather than over-decorated glassmorphism or heavy card spam.
- Color anchors: Primary `#00ce75` (success/action), Secondary `#ce0059` (warning/critical), Tertiary `#4f6dff` (supporting/interactive).
- Dark theme must derive from the exact same tonal ramps and brand identity as the light theme.

**Design Principles**
1. **Content Over Chrome**: The UI should recede, putting the language learning material center stage against quiet background surfaces.
2. **Intentional Interaction**: Strong focus states and predictable hover/pressed feedback are required. Avoid loud gradients or excessive borders. 
3. **Purposeful Motion**: Use quick, careful motion (e.g., subtle scaling on press). Always honor reduced-motion preferences.
4. **Accessible Consistency**: Enforce standard Apple-like target sizes (min 44px for primary actions), accessible contrast ratios, and structural rhythm across 375, 768, and 1440 widths.
